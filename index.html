Welcome to

Finance and Investments Org.

The Finance and Investments (FAI) Club looks to empower students with the knowledge and skills to navigate the corporate world. FAI has evolved into a thriving 501(c)(3) non-profit dedicated to fostering financial literacy and education among students worldwide.

Available Opportunities

For You

At FAI, resources are continuously built up to meet members’ desires. Our guest lectures continue each month, sharing some industry insights and allowing a special attending member Q&A. This year, chapters will share improved curriculum, featuring additional topics and interactive sessions. In addition, FAI is introducing exciting new competitions, namely virtual business challenges, entrepreneurship pitches, in addition to the annual stock market game. The introduction of FAI’s Mentor Pool will allow students to connect with seasoned business professionals, turning to them for specific 1-to-1 guidance and advice. Lastly, the Research Pipeline program will provide a one of a kind opportunity for upperclassmen to collaborate with finance/economics professors during hands-on research projects.
